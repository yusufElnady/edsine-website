EDSINE

Education and simulation for nuclear engineering

What is EDSINE?

EDSINE is a startup to develop a software to integrate different codes with the aim to simulate the operation of nuclear reactors in a virtual reality environment.



Why use nuclear reactor simulators?


Normally, educators use presentations, textbooks, and hard copies to deliver information to their students, but this method is proven to be tedious and boring for students, all while taking loads of effort from the educator.

On the other hand, virtual reality simulations allow the users to interact freely with many systems’ components of the nuclear reactors and safely test the impact of changing certain conditions.

3D models of nuclear power plant parts can be created and simulated dynamically to help the students understand how nuclear reactors work during normal and transient conditions in addition to observe the response of nuclear reactors during accident situations. The simulator can also be used in the training of personnel in the nuclear reactors to be familiar with reactor operation and to be able to deal with different circumstances along the reactors’ lifetime



Benefits

  • The simulator will help to train the employees in nuclear power plants to deal with the operation of the plant either in the normal or transient states.

  • The simulator will help in the education and training of the students in the field of nuclear power plants.

  • The simulator will address safety issues related to hypothetical nuclear power plant accidents.

  • The simulator will enhance the available workforce and global mobility of operators.

  • Development of nuclear engineering innovative tous for education, research and training is necessary to boost the development of qualified local labor in the field of nuclear power plants.
